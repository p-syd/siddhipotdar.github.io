About me

Hello! I am Siddhi, and I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Dallas. I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering from Savitribai Phule Pune University (formerly known as University of Pune) in 2021, after which I worked at a programmatic advertising company, PubMatic as a Machine Learning Engineer for real-time auctioning and ad inventory estimation. I also worked at Persistent Systems and myMO IT solutions as an intern during my undergraduate studies.

I love to explore and learn new technologies and frameworks in the field of machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. I find it fascinating to learn about the new algorithms in this space and their application to real-world use cases at scale.
